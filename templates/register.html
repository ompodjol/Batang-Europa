Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age requirement?

Players can register from age 8 onwards. We have programs for youth, junior, and senior categories.

When does the season start?

The regular season starts in September. Training camps begin in August. Check our schedule page for detailed dates.

Is previous experience required?

No! We welcome players of all skill levels. We have coaches to help you develop regardless of your experience.

What are the membership fees?

Please contact us for detailed pricing information. We offer scholarships for talented players with financial need.
